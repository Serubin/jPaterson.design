WALL-E

A remote controlled telepresence tank that connects an Oculus to a camera which rotates in sync with the user's head.
A VR telepresence tank controlled remotely through a Node.js server. We used a Raspberry Pi and Arduino on board to remotely connect an Oculus to a camera that rotates in sync with the user's head. Started at Treehacks, it has since been repurposed to become Adawarp and is currently funded by Rothenburg Ventures
Skills: Arduino, C#, Node.js, Oculus, Unity, Raspberry Pi

ISAACS

An open-source project to envision new ways for human users to intuitively interface and collaborate with aerial drones around augmented reality (AR) technologies.
ISAACS is an open-source project to envision new ways for human users to intuitively interface and collaborate with aerial drones around augmented reality (AR) technologies. ISAACS utilizes real-time SLAM solutions to localize the 3D coordinates of aerial vehicles and the operators wearing HoloLens. The first version of the platform will be developed in collaboration also with DJI based on the Matrice-100 platform.
Skills: C#, Unity, Drone controls, 3d Interface Design
